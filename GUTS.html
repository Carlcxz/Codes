Get Ready for the LANY World Tour!

LANY’s World Tour is an exciting chance for fans to experience the magic of the band live on stage. Known for their dreamy pop sound and heartfelt lyrics, LANY has captured the hearts of listeners with their unique blend of electronic and indie pop elements. The tour features a mix of fan favorites from albums like Malibu Nights and gg bb xx, along with new tracks that showcase the band’s growth and creativity.


As anticipation builds for the upcoming shows, fans can expect LANY's immersive live performances to create a deep connection with the audience. Frontman Paul Klein’s magnetic stage presence draws listeners in, making every concert feel personal despite the large crowds. The combination of stunning visuals, ambient lighting, and the band’s dynamic energy makes every show a unique experience.


For those attending the World Tour, it’s more than just a concert—it’s an experience. Fans will get to hear LANY’s latest tracks, feel the chemistry between band members, and sing along to their favorite songs. LANY’s tours are always highly anticipated, and this one is sure to live up to the excitement.

Featured Artists

LANY

LANY is an American indie pop band formed in 2014 in Los Angeles. The group is known for its dreamy, atmospheric sound, blending elements of pop, electronic, and R&B. The band's name, "LANY," is an acronym for "Los Angeles and New York," reflecting their connection to both cities. The band's core members are Paul Klein (vocals and guitar), Jake Goss (drums), and Les Priest (keyboard), although their live performances often feature additional musicians.

Khalid

American singer, songwriter, and record producer who gained widespread recognition with his breakthrough single "Location" in 2016. Known for his smooth, soulful voice and introspective lyrics, Khalid's music blends elements of R&B, pop, and electronic genres. His unique sound has resonated with a wide audience, particularly young listeners, thanks to his authentic storytelling and emotionally-driven tracks.

Julia Michaels

music is often characterized by its honest exploration of love, self-doubt, and personal growth. Her ability to write songs that are both catchy and emotionally resonant has earned her widespread acclaim. Over time, she has continued to release solo projects, including her 2019 album Inner Monologue Part 1, and has collaborated with numerous artists across genres, including LANY, Niall Horan, and JP Saxe. With her authentic approach to music and songwriting, Julia Michaels has carved out a distinctive place for herself in the pop landscape.
